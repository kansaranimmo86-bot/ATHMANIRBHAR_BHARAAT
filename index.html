About Swadeshi Fitness

Our Mission & Heritage

Our Mission

At Swadeshi Fitness, we're on a mission to make fitness accessible, affordable, and culturally relevant for every Indian. By combining India's rich fitness heritage with modern technology, we're creating solutions that resonate with Bharatiya values while addressing contemporary health challenges.

As student innovators, we believe in the power of Atmanirbhar Bharat - not just as a slogan, but as a practical approach to solving real problems with indigenous solutions.

Our Team

We're a passionate team of engineering and design students from IITs, NITs, and state universities across India. What unites us is our belief that India's fitness future should be built on our own soil, with our own hands.

Our Prototypes

Innovative Swadeshi Fitness Solutions

Fit India Band

Our affordable health tracker with 7-day battery life, heart rate monitoring, and Ayurvedic wellness insights.

  • Made with recycled materials
  • Hindi/English voice alerts
  • Under ₹1,499
  • Bharat-made sensors

Smart Mallakhamb

Traditional wooden pole with embedded sensors that provide real-time posture correction and performance analytics.

  • Teak wood from sustainable sources
  • Bluetooth connectivity
  • Works with AI Coach App
  • Preserves Indian heritage

Eco-Fit Cycle

Stationary bicycle made from recycled plastic and scrap metal, generating electricity while you workout!

  • 80% recycled materials
  • Powers your phone/lights
  • Low maintenance design
  • Made in local workshops

AI Coach App

Personal fitness coach in your pocket with voice guidance in 8 Indian languages and culturally relevant workouts.

  • Yoga, Surya Namaskar routines
  • Desi khel inspired workouts
  • Offline functionality
  • Community challenges
